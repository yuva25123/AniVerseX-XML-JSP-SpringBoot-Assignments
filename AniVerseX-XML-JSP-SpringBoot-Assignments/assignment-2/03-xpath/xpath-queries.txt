1) All anime titles
/animeCollection/anime/title/text()

2) Anime with rating > 8.5
/animeCollection/anime[number(rating) > 8.5]/title/text()

3) Anime with episodes less than 100
/animeCollection/anime[number(episodes) < 100]/title/text()

4) Anime by genre = Action
/animeCollection/anime[genre='Action']/title/text()

5) Count of anime records
count(/animeCollection/anime)

6) Title and shortReview
/animeCollection/anime/title | /animeCollection/anime/shortReview

7) animeId of highest rated anime
/animeCollection/anime[not(rating < /animeCollection/anime/rating)]/animeId/text()
